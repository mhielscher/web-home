Coilgun Sites

  • Barry's Coilgun Design Site - the absolute neccesity for beginners, and a great reference for more experienced coilgun addicts.
  • Magnetic Gun Club - a full-featured venture into coilguns and optimizing them.
  • George and David's Coilgun Page - The Eichinger coilgun site, with many pictures of their award-winning coilgun.
  • coilgun.eichingers.com Forum - The current coilgunners forum, since the demise of Wobot and subsequently the Yahoo coilgun group.
  • Coilgun.com - another example of coilguns. Some nice circuit schematics.
  • The Pskov 1100 - a well-made coilgun pistol, with a complete circuit diagram and some theory.
  • PowerLabs - lots of high-power stuff!
